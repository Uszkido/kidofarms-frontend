The Kido Genesis

Founded in 2020 by Usama Ado Shehu, Kido Farms & Orchard emerged from a singular realization: Nigeria's agricultural crisis isn't a lack of fertility, but a lack of Sovereign Infrastructure. Usama saw a future where technology doesn't just "help" farmers, but provides them with a full-stack Operating System to manage their destiny.

Core Mission

"To dismantle middleman exploitation and establish a Seed-to-Sovereignty digital pipeline where farmers own their data, their energy, and their market access."
