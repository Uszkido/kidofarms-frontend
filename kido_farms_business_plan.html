The Economic Model

Revenue Streams

  • Marketplace Fee: 10% commission on direct-to-consumer sales.
  • Basket Nodes: Recurring subscription revenue from hotels and institutions.
  • Energy Credits: Revenue from biomass conversion certification.
  • Insurance Premiums: Yield-Shield micro-insurance service fees.

Target Market

  • Urban Prosumers: Health-conscious city dwellers in Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt.
  • Production Nodes: Over 10 million smallholder farmers in the West African sub-region.
  • Export Gate: International buyers seeking traceable organic produce.
