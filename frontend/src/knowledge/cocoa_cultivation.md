# Cocoa Cultivation Protocol (Ondo/Osun)
## Optimal Environment
Cocoa requires high humidity and well-drained loamy soils rich in organic matter. Best performance is seen in the "Cocoa Belt" of South West Nigeria.

## Shade Management
Plant plantain or banana trees as temporary shade for young cocoa seedlings to prevent direct sun scorch.

## Pest Mitigation (Organic)
Use neem seed oil sprays to manage *Sahlbergella singularis* (Cocoa Mirids). Apply during early morning hours.

## Fermentation Quality
Ferment beans in tiered wooden boxes for 5-7 days for optimal flavor development. Turn every 48 hours.
