# Kido Protocol: Organic Pest & Disease Management
## Overview
Organic pest control at Kido Farms relies on biological synergy rather than synthetic chemicals. This protocol outlines natural remedies for common West African crop threats.

## Common Pests & Organic Solutions
### 1. Aphids & Whiteflies
- **Neem Oil Spray**: Mix 5ml of pure neem oil with 2ml of liquid soap in 1 liter of water. Spray every 7 days.
- **Garlic-Chili Fusion**: Blend 2 bulbs of garlic with 5 hot peppers. Steep in 1 liter of water overnight. Strain and spray to repel soft-bodied insects.

### 2. Stem Borers (Maize/Sorghum)
- **Push-Pull Strategy**: Intercrop maize with Desmodium (repels borers) and plant Napier grass around the field (attracts borers away from crops).

### 3. Root-Knot Nematodes
- **Marigold Intercropping**: Plant African Marigolds alongside vegetables. Marigold roots release alpha-terthienyl, which is toxic to nematodes.

## Disease Mitigation
### 1. Fungal Blight
- **Baking Soda Solution**: Mix 1 tablespoon of baking soda with 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil and 1 liter of water.
- **Wood Ash**: Sprinkle clean wood ash around the base of plants to manage soil acidity and deter fungal spores.

## Soil Testing
- Always verify soil pH before application. Kido Premium standards require a pH between 6.2 and 7.2 for optimal natural pest resilience.
