# Kido Protocol: Poultry & Livestock Management (Tropical Focus)
## Overview
Standard operating procedures for managing poultry and small ruminants within the Kido regenerative ecosystem.

## Broiler & Layer Care
### 1. Heat Stress Management
- In high-temperature regions (e.g., Kano, Sokoto), ensure adequate ventilation. 
- Use "Evaporative Cooling": Hang wet jute bags near ventilation points during peak heat (12pm - 4pm).
- Add electrolytes to water during heatwaves.

### 2. Organic Feeding Protocols
- Supplement commercial feed with high-protein local greens: Moringa leaves, Azolla, and Sunflower seeds.
- Black Soldier Fly (BSF) Larvae: Implement BSF bins for 100% organic waste-to-protein conversion for poultry.

## Disease Prevention (Bio-Security)
- **Footbaths**: Maintain disinfectant footbaths (Lime/Potassium Permanganate) at every poultry entrance.
- **Quarantine**: New birds must be isolated for 14 days before joining the main flock.
- **Essential Oils**: Use Oregano oil in water to boost immune response against Coccidiosis.

## Ruminant Care (Goats & Sheep)
- **Rotational Grazing**: Divide land into paddocks. Move livestock every 4 days to prevent overgrazing and break parasite cycles.
- **Salt Licks**: Provide mineral blocks to prevent deficiencies common in savannah regions.
