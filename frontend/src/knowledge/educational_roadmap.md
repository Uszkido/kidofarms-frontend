# Kido Mastery Academy: Educational Roadmap (2025-2026)
## Overview
The Kido Mastery Academy is the educational wing of Kido Farms, dedicated to transforming traditional farmers into "Sovereign Agronomists." The curriculum is divided into specialized modules designed for regional West African contexts.

## 1. Foundation Level: The Bio-Digital Basic
*   **Module 101: Soil Health & Bio-Chemistry**: Understanding pH balance, nitrogen fixing, and organic composting techniques.
*   **Module 102: The Digital Ledger**: How to use the Kido Citizen Dashboard to track harvests and manage wallet transactions.
*   **Module 103: Parametric Insurance Fundamentals**: Understanding how "Yield-Shield" automatically triggers payouts based on satellite weather data.

## 2. Professional Level: Specialist Pathways
*   **Master Orchardist (Cocoa/Cashew focus)**: Techniques for high-yield pruning, pest management using neem oil, and fermentation excellence.
*   **Logistics Node Operator**: Training for carriers and distributors on cold-chain management and cluster-batching efficiency.
*   **Precision Irrigation Specialist**: Learning to deploy and maintain solar-powered drip systems integrated with Kido's real-time moisture sensors.

## 3. Sovereign Level: The Orbit Mastery
*   **Carbon Credit Architect**: Certifying farm waste for the Sovereign Energy Marketplace.
*   **Export Compliance Officer**: Mastering the standards for global trade (EU/US/Asia) to unlock premium pricing for Kido-certified produce.
*   **Neural Node Trainer**: Helping refine the Kido AI Concierge by providing localized crop symptoms and regional language nuances.

## Certification
Graduates receive a "Heritage Certificate" on the Kido Blockchain, unlocking higher credit limits and priority access to export batches.
