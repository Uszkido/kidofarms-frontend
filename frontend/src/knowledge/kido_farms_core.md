# Kido Farms: Core Sovereign Architecture
## Overview
Kido Farms is a Bio-Digital Neural Network designed for West African agricultural sovereignty. It integrates e-commerce, parametric insurance (Yield-Shield), and energy-positive waste management.

## Mission
To eliminate middleman exploitation by digitizing the "Seed-to-Sovereignty" pipeline, providing farmers with direct market access and financial resilience.

## Core Components
*   **The Market Grid**: Direct-from-source marketplace for Citizens.
*   **Heritage ID**: A unique biometric/digital ID for every crop, tracking it from soil to shelf.
*   **Yield-Shield**: Parametric insurance triggered by weather data sensors.
*   **Sovereign Energy Marketplace**: Converting biomass waste into Carbon Credits and Energy Tokens.
*   **Mastery Academy**: Educational wing for training "Sovereign Agronomists."

## Governance & Security
*   **Orbit 5.0**: The Administrative Mission Control.
*   **Ghost Protocol**: Admin impersonation for seamless troubleshooting.
*   **Global Data Command**: Tiered permissions for managing the root ledger.
*   **Trust Oracle**: AI-driven risk scoring for Citizen credit limits.

## Technology Stack
*   **Frontend**: Next.js 16 + Framer Motion.
*   **Backend**: Node.js + Express + Drizzle ORM.
*   **Database**: Neon Serverless PostgreSQL.
*   **AI**: Groq (Llama 3.3) + Gemini 1.5 Flash.

## Architectural Philosophy: "Always Bloom Logic"
Ensuring system resilience and functionality even in low-bandwidth or high-latency environments. The farmer is always at the center of the sovereignty model.
