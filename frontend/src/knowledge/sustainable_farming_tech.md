**Classification: Sovereign Agricultural Intelligence**

## 1. Smart Farming & Plant Parameters (OBIA4RTM)
Precision farming requires the retrieval of plant parameters using radiative transfer models.
*   **Method**: Use object-based image analysis to interpret canopy health.
*   **Kido Node**: Our Crop Sensors (GPS/Mesh) should be calibrated to detect chlorophyll density and water stress using these spectral models.

## 2. Soil Health Indexing (Open Bodem Index - OBI)
Evaluation of agricultural soil must be holistic, covering:
*   **Chemical**: pH, organic matter, nutrient balance.
*   **Physical**: Structure, water-holding capacity, compaction.
*   **Biological**: Microbial activity and biodiversity.
*   **Action**: Use the OBI calculator patterns to generate "Soil Vitality Scores" for Kido member farms.

## 3. Agroecosystem Modeling (DSSAT & Cycles)
To predict yields and climate impact, we utilize time-step models.
*   **Management**: 
    - **Satellite Biomass Analysis**: Using Sentinel-2 multispectral data to calculate NDVI (biomass) and NDWI (water stress) for precise fertilizer and irrigation application.
    - Simulating biophysical processes (Nitrogen cycle, Carbon turnover) allows for precise fertilizer application.
*   **Resilience**: Use these models to assess the impact of regional climate variability in the Sahel and coastal regions.

## 4. Carbon Footprint Audit (Protocol: NP-Carbon-CALC)

The Sovereign Grid implements a high-precision emission monitoring protocol for supply chain logistics and energy consumption.

### A. Transportation Emission Factors (g CO2/km)
- **Motorcycle (Kido Express)**: 80g
- **Van (Medium Courier)**: 220g
- **Truck (Heavy Logistics)**: 450g
- **Bicycle (Local Node)**: 0g

### B. Energy Nodes (kg CO2/kWh)
- **Solar (Primary Grid)**: 0.04kg
- **Gas Generator (Backup)**: 0.85kg
- **Diesel Pump**: 2.6kg per liter

### C. Logic Synthesis
Total Emissions (kg) = Σ (Distance_i * Factor_i) / 1000.
Administrators must use the 'Environmental Audit' dashboard to provision carbon credits based on verified delivery efficiency.
