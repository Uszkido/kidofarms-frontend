# Ginger Production Excellence (Kaduna)
## Location Focus
Southern Kaduna produces the world's most pungent ginger, particularly the "UG-1" variety.

## Soil Preparation
Ginger is a heavy feeder. Integrate 20 tonnes of cured poultry manure per hectare before planting.

## Planting Nodes
Use rhizomes with at least two or three "eyes." Plant at a depth of 5-10cm.

## Post-Harvest Curing
Dry ginger in the shade to maintain the essential oleoresin content. Do not peel completely to preserve the "Nigerian Punch" flavor profile.
