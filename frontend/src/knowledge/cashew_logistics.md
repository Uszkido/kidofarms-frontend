# Cashew Hub Logistics (Kogi/Benue)
## Harvesting Window
Peak harvest occurs between February and May. Only harvest nuts that have fallen naturally to ensure maximum oil content (CNSL).

## Sorting Standards
Export-grade nuts must have a kernel out-turn ratio (KOR) of 48lbs or higher.

## Storage
Store in breathable jute bags on wooden pallets. Moisture content must be maintained below 9% to prevent fungal growth.

## Kido Traceability
All cashew batches must be tagged with the "Kido-ID" originating from the specific farm cluster for transparency.
