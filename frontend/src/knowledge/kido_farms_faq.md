# Kido Farms: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is Kido Farms?
Kido Farms is a Sovereign Food Operating System. It is an integrated platform for agricultural trade, education, and financial sovereignty for West African farmers.

### How do I join the Kido Academy?
Citizens can access the Academy via the "Mastery" tab in their dashboard. Completing modules like "Soil Health" or "Organic Pest Control" increases your Trust Score.

### What are Energy Tokens?
Energy Tokens are digital assets earned when you convert organic farm waste (biomass) into energy or fertilizer via Kido-certified technical nodes.

### How does "Yield-Shield" work?
It is a parametric insurance system. If Kido sensors detect extreme weather (drought or flood in your region), a payout is automatically triggered to your Kido Wallet without the need for manual claims.

### What is a Heritage ID?
It is a unique identifier assigned to every harvest. It links the produce to the specific farmer, the soil chemistry reports, and the exact harvest timestamp, ensuring 100% transparency.

### Who can become an Admin?
Only users with "Global Data Command" clearance can access the full Orbit 5.0 suite. All administrative actions are recorded in the immutable Activity Logs.

### Is Kido Farms only for farmers?
No. Kido Farms is for Consumers (Citizens), Businesses (Hotels/Restaurants), Carriers (Logistics), and Farmers. It is a unified ecosystem for the entire food chain.
