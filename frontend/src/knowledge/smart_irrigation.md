# Smart Irrigation & Water Management
## Dry Season Survival
In Northern Nigeria, dry season farming (Fadama) is critical. Use drip irrigation nodes to conserve water by 40% compared to flood irrigation.

## Scheduling
Water crops only during the hours of 6:00 AM - 8:00 AM or after 6:00 PM to eliminate evaporative loss.

## Rainwater Harvesting
Kido Farm Clusters should implement sand-dams and plastic-lined reservoirs to capture the first rains in March.

## Salinity Check
Monitor borehole water salinity monthly. High salt content will stunt the growth of leafy greens like Spinach and Ugu.
